KEY to R scripts for data analysis

ac~age.R 
    Model the effects of age on acoustics

ac~cause.R
    Acoustic differences between crying based on what caused it

ac~pairs.R
    Pair plots - correlations between acoustic characteristics per baby across ages (eg some babies consistenty have high pitch relative to their age peers)

ac~sex.R
    Model the effects of age on acoustics

cause~ac.R
    Random Forest models for predicting the cause of crying from acoustics

descriptives_sumtab.R
    Summary of the dataset

indivDif.R
    Individual recognition with Random Forest models

preps.R
    Preparation, segmentation, acoustic analysis

umap_helperFun.R
    Helper functions for UMAP, especially for computing a distance matrix based on DTW of frame-by-frame features, running in parallel on multiple cores

umap_per_age.R
    A separate distance matrix for each age group

umap_per_cause.R
    A separate distance matrix for each cause of crying

zz_formatting.R
    Variable names for plotting, helper functions

playbacks.R
    Analysis of the playbacks results
